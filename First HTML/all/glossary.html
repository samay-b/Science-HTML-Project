physics defined list

Force
Strength classified as a physical action or movement.
Motion
The action of an object changing its position due to forces.
Position
A place where someone or something is located or has been put.
Frame of Reference
A stated point in relation to judgements of motion can be made.
Direction
A course along which a person or object moves towards.
Distance
An amount of space between two people, places, or things.
Speed
The rate of someone's or something's motion or operation.
Time
A measurement of a period of existance that passed by.
Vector
A quantity of direction along with magnitude.
Velocity
A vector which consists of speed and direction.
Acceleration
The absolute change of velocity of something, mostly speed.
Momentum
The impetus, or force of energy gained by a moving object.
Mass
The amount of matter that an object contains within itself.
Weight
The amount of heaviness that a person or object contains.
Gravity
A force attracting two bodies having mass towards each other.
Friction
The force resisting motion by rubbing of surfaces, creating heat.
Inertia
A property of motion where an object's tendency to stay in its state of motion and direction will only be altered by an external force.
